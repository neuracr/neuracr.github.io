Instructions

  1. Cut your half lime in 4 and put it in the glass with the raspberries.
  2. Remove the mint leaves from the stem and put it in the glass. You can leave out the head for decoration.
  3. Pour the sugar syrup in the glass
  4. Crush the content of the glass with a muddler.
  5. Add the ice, the rum, the sparkling water and the brown sugar.
  6. Stir the content of the glass and add the straw and the mint stem.
For a classic mojito you can leave out the raspberries and take a full lime.

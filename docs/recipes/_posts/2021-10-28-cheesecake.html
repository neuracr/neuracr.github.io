  2. Put baking paper at the bottom of the cake pan with removable bottom and butter the side

  1. Soak 4 gelatin leaves in cold water for 5 min. Keep some of the fresh cream to soak the last leaf of gelatin later
  2. In a container mix the rest of the fresh cream, the ricotta and all of the sugar
  3. In a pot heat the fresh cream and dissolve the 4 gelatin leaves. Add the mixture with the cream and ricotta. Mix thoroughly
  4. Spread over the cookie and put it in the fridge for 2 hours before adding the raspberry topping

  1. Soak the last gelatin leaf in cold water

  3. Add the gelatin to the raspberries and mix it
  4. Pour the topping over the cake and sway the cake pan to spread over the whole surface

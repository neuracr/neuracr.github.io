Instructions

  1. Coarsely chop the pork, the carrots and the celery. Mince the onion
  2. In a big pot brown the onion approximately 5 minutes
  3. Lower the heat, add the meat and the paprika, simmer covered 6 to 10 minutes
  4. Add the vegetables and if you want the cream. Fill the pot with water to the level of the vegetables and meat.
  5. Simmer covered for at least an hour and stir from time to time
  6. Before the end of the cooking add the cumin and seasoning
  7. In your plate add the fresh coriander and if you want pour some parmesan
You can serve with potatoes, semolina or pasta.

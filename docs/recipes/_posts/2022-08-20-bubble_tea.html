The default quantities correspond to 1L of bubble tea for 4 small glasses or 3 big glasses. Cooking time: 30 min, Total time: 1h 15min

Instructions

  1. In a pot boil the first volume of water
  2. In a kettle boil the second volume of water
  3. In a jug pour 4/5 of the second volume (75cL with default values). Infuse the tea bags for 7 min.
  4. Once the water is boiling in the pot, put in the bobas and boil them for 30min (the time depends on the bobas you have)
  5. In a bowl pour the 1/5 of hot water remaining, add the honey and the sugar and stir until complete dissolution
  6. Once the tea infused remove the bags and add the milk. To make the tea cool faster you can make a cold bain-marie in your sink.
  7. Once the bobas boiled remove the pot from the heat and wait for 20min. Put the jug and the bowl in the fridge.
  8. Once the bobas cooled sieve the bobas with cold water and then put them in the bowl with sugar and honey and wait for 5min.
  9. In a glass or a cup pour the tea (3/4 of your cup), add the bobas with as much sugar as you want and add 3 ice cubes.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C
  2. Cut the tomatoes into slices and the ham into squares
  3. Roll out the puff pastry in your pie dish and heat it for 10 min
  4. Remove the pastry from the oven and spread the following ingredients in that order : mustard, cream, ham, tomato and grated cheese
  5. Bake the pie for 15-20 min
